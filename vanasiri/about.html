About Vanasiri

Our story of bringing nature closer to you

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Vanasiri began with a simple mission: to make the beauty and benefits of plants accessible to everyone. What started as a small local nursery has grown into a trusted destination for plant enthusiasts across India.

We believe that every home deserves the life-giving presence of plants. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just beginning your plant journey, we're here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality plants and exceptional customer service, helping people create beautiful, healthy living spaces filled with nature's beauty.

Our Vision

To become the leading plant retailer in India, inspiring a greener, more sustainable future through the power of plants.

Our Values

Quality

We source only the finest plants from trusted growers and nurseries.

Trust

Building lasting relationships with our customers through honesty and transparency.

Sustainability

Committed to eco-friendly practices and promoting environmental consciousness.

Community

Supporting local growers and fostering a community of plant lovers.
